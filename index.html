From foundational concepts to complex attack methodologies, these books equip readers with the knowledge and skills to identify and exploit vulnerabilities in systems and networks. Serious Cryptography by Jean-Philippe Aumasson

Serious Cryptography by Jean-Philippe Aumasson is a comprehensive and practical guide to modern encryption. Aimed at both beginners and experienced practitioners, the book provides a clear and accessible explanation of the mathematical foundations of cryptography without overwhelming readers with complex formulas.   Aumasson covers essential topics such as authenticated encryption, secure randomness, hash functions, block ciphers, and public-key techniques like RSA and elliptic curve cryptography. He also emphasizes the importance of practical implementation and highlights potential pitfalls to avoid.   With its focus on real-world applications and a clear writing style, Serious Cryptography is an invaluable resource for anyone looking to gain a solid understanding of modern encryption techniques.
